Key Risks & Mitigations

📅
Weekend dead zone (Business Park)

Office parks lose ~40% of foot traffic on Sat/Sun. Mitigation: prioritise weekday stock depth; run weekend-specific promos for nearby Fantastico/Lidl shoppers passing through.

💰
Price sensitivity (46% of survey)

Nearly half of respondents cited high prices as their #1 barrier. The tiered strategy partially addresses this — but entry-tier items must be visibly priced at 3–5 BGN to attract repeat daily visits.

🐢
Slow awareness ramp (Month 1–2)

No marketing budget in current plan. Recommend 200–300 BGN on local Instagram/Facebook ads targeted at Mladost 4 coordinates in Month 1. Pays back within Week 2 of Month 2.

🥗
Fresh SKU waste in early months

Ready salads/sandwiches (top survey demand) have short shelf life. Start with small fresh stock batches in Month 1–2, then scale. Use the −40% same-day markdown to drive sell-through.

🏦
Loan repayment pressure

Interest-only cost is ~38 BGN/mo — very manageable. Confirm with lender whether principal repayments start Month 1 or Month 7+. If Month 1, add ~150–200 BGN/mo to fixed costs, pushing breakeven to Month 9–10.
